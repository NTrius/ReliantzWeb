Our Team

Sid Ferrales

CEO

Sid has served as a senior executive building and scaling successful global businesses. As an expert in human capital and organization development, Sid has held corporate level positions at such companies as Motorola, Digital Equipment and Dell Technologies as well as medium size businesses such as RealNetworks and Micron Electronics. Most recently, he served as CHRO at CLEAResult, the premier leading energy efficiency consulting company. CLEAResult was recently named by Forbes as one America’s Best Mid-Size Employers of 2019.

Sid’s passion is engendering positive, productive environments by helping people reach their full potential. He first acted on this motivation early in his career by counseling troubled youth. He has maintained this focus throughout his career, from helping build Dell into a Fortune 500 business and serving as an adviser to such companies as RealNetworks and Napster. Sid belongs to the Society for Human Resource Management as well as a member of HR People + Strategy. Sid holds an MA from Sam Houston State University specializing in social rehabilitation and a BA in Sociology from Texas State University.

Cindy Thompson

Practice Lead

Total Rewards & HR Systems

Cindy is the consummate Human Resources professional who demonstrates executive presence, deep domain expertise and strong interpersonal skills. She has demonstrated the ability to partner and work effectively with Executive Teams as well as at the Board of Director level. Cindy has experience as an HR Business Partner and in Talent Acquisition, but her expertise is in Total Rewards and HR Systems. She also has extensive expertise in acquisitions including diligence and integration. While at CLEAResult she was an integral part of six acquisitions within an 18-month timeframe. As a Senior Director at CLEAResult, Cindy was selected to serve for a term on the Operating Committee of the Company. Cindy most recently completed a 9-month assignment at Paycom working on aligning compensation practices to the company business objectives. Cindy enjoys learning about businesses and entrepreneurs. She is a published author of entrepreneur stories, “A Cup of Cappuccino for the Entrepreneur’s Spirit, Volume II”.

Cindy practices the philosophy that the needs of the business and the needs of the employees are not mutually exclusive, and strives to position HR organizations to strategically support business objectives. Cindy holds a Bachelor of Science degree from Oklahoma State University.

Chris Ferrales

Director of Operations & Practice Lead for

Core HR Services & Change Management

Chris’ multidimensional career brings clients a wealth of strategic and pragmatic business expertise. His expertise includes executive and front line management team building and coaching, organization development, change management, M&A, and international HR.

Spanning a 25 year career in human resources, Chris has held leadership positions at large firms such as Dell Technologies and medium-sized companies including Quest Software. Chris has held positions in a variety of industries (think tank, non-profit, finance, high-tech) and organizations (small tech startups, multinational Fortune 500). As an essential adviser and member of numerous leadership teams, he has helped manage workforces through inflection points and facilitated business success through periods of dramatic growth.

Chris holds a Master’s degree in Learning and Organizational Change from Northwestern University in Evanston, IL and a Bachelor’s degree in Sociology from the University of California in Berkeley, CA.

Our Approach

We take a strengths-based approach to working with executive teams so that leaders are contributing to their highest level in the right roles in order to meet the strategic needs of the business.

Our view is that talent and culture form an ecosystem, that properly planned and managed, contributes both to the health of the company as well as its people’s work and life.

Experience has taught us that employee engagement fuels the fire behind any successful company’s achievements.

Whether you are an experienced and seasoned executive, or in your first C-suite job trying to manage a fast-paced startup, you will benefit from the consultative expertise offered by Reliantz, especially in our increasingly complex, diverse and potentially litigious business environment.

Who Benefits from Our Experience

  • Organizations who need a talent strategy to support their company’s business plans.
  • Companies preparing to acquire or merge with another organization or pursue additional funding – they need a “talent story” to demonstrate their human capital value.
  • Leadership teams seeking to add and integrate c-suite leaders to manage growth strategies.
  • Teams who lack senior level HR leadership, needing interim consultative expertise to shore up their HR function.
  • Businesses that need to ensure their HR practices and policies are compliant and need fundamental management training to protect them in a litigious and complex work world.
